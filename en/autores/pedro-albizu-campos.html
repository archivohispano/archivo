--- layout: author title: Pedro Albizu Campos author_name: Pedro Albizu Campos author_id: pedro-albizu-campos birth_date: 1891 death_date: 1965 country: Puerto Rico lang: en bio: | Pedro Albizu Campos (1891-1965) was a Puerto Rican attorney and politician, leader of the Puerto Rican Nationalist Party and the most prominent figure in the Puerto Rican independence movement of the 20th century. A graduate of Harvard Law School, Albizu Campos dedicated his life to the struggle for Puerto Rican independence. His political thought combined nationalism with a profound reflection on Latin American cultural identity.
